Pushtimarg doesn't have agamic worship. They have strict bifurcation of vedic worship and pushti worship. While doing vedic duties they may offer oblations to other deities but while doing pushti seva they only krsna. As the founder of the sect himself says that Only Adi Murti Krsna can be served in pushtimarg not even other forms. Apart from krsna his consorts like yamuna also receive seva.
