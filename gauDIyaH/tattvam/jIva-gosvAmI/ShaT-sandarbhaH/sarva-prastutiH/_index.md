<details><summary>विश्वास-टिप्पनी</summary>

Early bits of ShaT-sandarbha seem to have been based on SV-raised gopAlabhaTTa's lost work - quotes shrIbhAShya 1.1 quite a bit; and vAdikesarI-saumya/sundara-jAmAtR-muni is reverentially cited in paramAtma-sandarbha.

In later parts, there are no rAmAnujIya citations. So, they're likely heavy in jIva-gosvAmin's independent contributions.
