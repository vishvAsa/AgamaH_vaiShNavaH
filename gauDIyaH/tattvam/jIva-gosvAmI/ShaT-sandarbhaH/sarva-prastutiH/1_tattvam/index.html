The numbering has been adjusted slightly. The old numbering system has been kept, but the following should be noted. The Tattva-sandarbha differs from the other five sandarbhas in that it is divided into two sections‚Äîthe PramƒÅ·πáa-prakara·πáam and the Prameya-prakara·πáam. The first section establishes the authority of the BhƒÅgavatam as a source of revealed knowledge or shabda. The second enters into the content of the BhƒÅgavata-sandarbha. Thus the numbering system followed throughout the rest of the BhƒÅgavata-sandarbha only begins with the Prameya-prakara·πáam, with each section corresponding to the main BhƒÅgavata verse under discussion. This is apparently what Puridas followed in the numbering in his Sarva-samvƒÅdinƒ´ edition. Baladeva is the apparent source of the current numbering system, which he himself announces at the end of the commentary to the first verse.
