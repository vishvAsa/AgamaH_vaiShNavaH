Bhakti Is the Most Auspicious Path Even for Brahma-siddhi 
४६ | श्रीकापिलेयेऽपि यथाह ( भा० ३।२५।१९ ) - 
THE SAME point [that bhakti is the abhidheya] is also made by Kapila in His teachings: 
न युज्यमानया भक्त्या भगवत्यखिलात्मनि । 
सदृशोऽस्ति शिवः पन्था योगिनां ब्रह्मसिद्धये ॥ ६९ ॥ 
For yogis striving for perfection in Brahman realization, there is no other path as auspicious as bhakti performed for the sake of Bhagavan, the Immanent Self of all beings. (SB 3.25.19)' 
ब्रह्मसिद्धिः परतत्त्वाविर्भावः ॥ 
The compound brahma-siddhi, "perfection in Brahman realization," means "the self-disclosure of the Supreme Reality" (paratattva-āvirbhāva).
