An interesting aspect of the pA~ncharAtrika sanatkumAra saMhitA is the vyUha-wise incarnation of avatAra-s. As per its theory, not all are from nArAyana, but are derived from different vyUha-s and viShvaksena. The avatAra-s it recognizes are: 

1. varAha (←aniruddha); 
2. nR^isiMha (←aniruddha); 
3. matsya (←saMkarShaNa), associated with the slaying of madhu and kaiTabha whole stole the veda; 
4. kUrma akUpAra (←saMkarShaNa); 
5. tArkShya (←viShvaksena); 
6. vAmana (←nArAyaNa == vAsudeva); 
7. hayagrIva (←pradyumna); 
8. kapila (←saMkarShaNa) 
9. jAmadagnya (←saMkarShaNa); 
10 rAma (lost; manusript lacunose); 
11. sAttvata heroes (the combined incarnation of all the vyUha-s). devakIputra is said to have promulgated p~ancharAtra during this incarnation to his followers.
