<details><summary>दीक्षादि-प्रयोगः</summary>

> A small set of people in mysuru. Till late 1700s the chamunda temple had priests following lakshmi tantra. The temple faced some issues during tipu rule and post tipu, the temple was in disuse. Krishnaraja wodeyar iii was the one who brought TN arcakas to the temple back then and they are continuing till now. A mandyam guy whose family had some ties with the temple in the past stated about this. They lost rights only after the restoration which was done using TN priests, it seems. - RL


> There was a tradition of specifically Lakshmi tantra followers existing in the 19th- 20th century, but it has got extinct. - तीक्ष्णदंष्ट्रः।
