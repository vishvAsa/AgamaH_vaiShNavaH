haḥ prakalpayet ||82||

vāmopari gadā kāryā śaṅkhaṃ cād-
haḥ suśobhanam |
etallakṣaṇasaṃyuktā pratimā śub-
hadā bhavet ||83||

śrī-pūṣṭī cāpi kartavye
padmavīnākarānvite |
ūrumātrocchritāyāme kiñcidāvarji-
te tathā ||84||

nānāvidyādharau kāryau prabhā-
maṇḍalasaṃsthitau |
prabhāṃ vibhūṣayedatnādgajavyā-
lādibhirbudhaḥ ||85||

pādābha-pādapiṭhañca tathā śob-
hāsamanvitam |
deśaveśānurūpeṇa kartavyaṃ deśa-
kottamaiḥ ||86||

atha śrīgopāladevasya kaścidvi-
śeṣaḥ viṣṇudharmottare—

gopālapratimāṃ kuryādveṇuvāda-
natatparām |
varhapiñchaṃ ghanasyāmāṃ dvibhu-
jāmūrdhvasaṃsthitām ||87||

veṇuvādana-tat-parāṃ iti—śrī-
mad-adhara-nyasta-veṇuvādana-
mandra-śobhitām | śrī-hasta-dvaya-
dhṛta-sahaja-sanilagnaiva vaṃśī vid-
heyetyarthaḥ | ūrdhva-saṃsthitāmiti

…

garuḍāsanagaṇāṃ devaṃ prathame
vayasi sthitam || 270||

cakraṃ khaḍgañca musalaṃ
śaṅkhuśaṃ dakṣiṇe nyaset |
śaṅkhaṃ śārṅgaṃ gadaṃ pāśaṃ
vāmahasteṣu vinyaset |
lakṣmī sarasvatī kārye
padmavīnāsamanvite || 271||

kiñca pratiṣṭhāprasange—
aṣṭabāhuḥ pūrvameva kathitaḥ pu-
ruṣottamaḥ |
caturbāhuṃ dvibāhuñca saṃkṣepāt
kathayāmi te || 272||

dakṣiṇaṃ śāntidaṃ hastaṃ kuryāda-
yaṃ dayānvitaṃ |
vāmorddhvaṃ kārayeccakraṃ pāṃ-
cajanyaṃ tathā hyadhaḥ ||273||
śrīpuṣṭisahitaṃ kuryāccaturbāhuṃ
surottamaṃ |
prasāde sthāpayennityaṃ gṛhe vā
maṇḍape'pi vā ||274||

atha dvibāhuṃ taṃ devaṃ kuryād-
bhedadvayena tu |
ekaṃ śāntikaraṃ hastamaparaṃ
cakrabhūṣitaṃ |
śrī sarasvatī saṃyutamekāmāyatane
nyaset ||275||

gadācakradharaṃ vāpi dvibhujaṃ
puruṣottamaṃ | sirapāṇisamopeta-
mekānaṃśānvitaṃ vibhum |
