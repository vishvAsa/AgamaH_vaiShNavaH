## शास्त्रम्
> the actual exact reference to the size, shape of the Urdhvapundram in Vaikhanasa texts, is found in this book RAMADESIKHAANIKHAM . the naamam is supposed to be "shuurpaakaram " (shape of a winnowing fan) and "chaturaasraangulam" in its width , with sri pada renu reaching the sahasra-chakra from ajna chakra. - मानसः

> Tirumala arcaka Ramana Dikshitulu also stated that the shape of namam as per their texts would be like vdk only. Like a padam. Not two feet. Ramana dikshitulu said their text talks about shape of foot only. No dvivacana or padadvayam. - RL
