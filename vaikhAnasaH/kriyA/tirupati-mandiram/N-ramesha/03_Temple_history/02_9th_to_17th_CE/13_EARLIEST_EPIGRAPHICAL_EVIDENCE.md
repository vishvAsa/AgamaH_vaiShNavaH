The earliest inscription found in the Tirumala temple mentions the birth of the Bana prince, named Vijayaditya, who flourished in the early half of the ninth century (429-T.T.). This prince is said to have made a gift to Lord Venkatesvara at Tiruchanur. (229-G.T.).
