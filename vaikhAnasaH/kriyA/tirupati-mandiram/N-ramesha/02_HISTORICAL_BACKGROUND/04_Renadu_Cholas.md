title = "04 Renadu Cholas"
+++

About the period of the Chalukyas of Badami and the Greater Pallavas  of Kanchi, a portion of this district was under the rule of a branch of the  Cholas claiming descent from the family of the historical or quasi-historical  figure Karikala Chola. These chiefs are called the Renadu Cholas after the  tract Renadu 7000 in Cuddapah district. Of these chiefs, the inscriptions  of Mahendravikrama known by his title 'Chola Maharaja' and his son  Punyakumara are found in this district.
