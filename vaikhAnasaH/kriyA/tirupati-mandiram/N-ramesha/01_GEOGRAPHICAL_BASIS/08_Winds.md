title = "32 Winds"
+++

Winds are generally light and blow mainly from directions between  south-west and north-west during the period from May to September; for  the rest of the year, winds are light and variable in the morning. Afternoon winds are stronger, and in the period from October to January, they are mainly  north westerly or easterly; in the next three months, afternoon winds are mostly  from direction between east and north
