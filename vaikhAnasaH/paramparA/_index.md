## द्वारका-तिरुपति-देवालयः
In east Godavari district ( called Chinna tirupati among telugu people).

> Late Agnihotram shrInivAsAcharyulu was considered as a living legend among vaikhanasas. he was the erstwhile head of dwaraka Tirumala veda pathasala , but then due to his proficiency , he was specially invited by TTD to be the dean of venkateswara veda Vidyalaya.


## ग्रन्थ-प्रचारः
4 bodies for publishing vaikhanasa grantha s.

1. TTD
2. vikhanasa trust, tirumala, closely aligned but seperate from TTD
3. dwaraka tirumala
4. akulamannadu ( some vaikhanasa scholars live there)
